sf.shop Online Shopping Application UX Case Study

This UX case study focuses on addressing challenges encountered by online shoppers, particularly those using the Cash on Delivery (COD) payment method, such as concerns about security, lack of transparency, and inflexibility in canceling orders. By applying design thinking principles and UX methodologies, we aim to understand user needs, define problem areas, and propose solutions to enhance the online shopping experience. Our goal is to prioritize user reassurance, transparency, and flexibility, ultimately creating a more secure and empowering environment for all online shoppers.

Ideabox Platform UX Research Case Study

Brian Steve's UX research case study for Ideabox platform at Telkom Digital Amoeba highlights challenges in user engagement and conversion. Analyzing user data over three months reveals significant increases in total users but a decline in returning users, signaling retention issues. Usability testing identifies confusion and frustration in the idea submission journey, prompting recommendations for clearer instructions, streamlined processes, and real-time feedback to improve user experience and retention.
